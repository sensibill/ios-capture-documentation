Compatibility

Development Environment

  • Xcode 11.0 - 12.0.1
  • Swift runtime 5.1 - 5.3

Deployment

  • iPhone devices running on 64-bit architecture (iPhone 5S or newer).
  • iOS 11.0 - 14.0

Notes:

  • Blur detection during capture is not supported on iPhone 6 or lower models
